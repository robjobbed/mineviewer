# MineViewer Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

## Overview

MineViewer is a free, open-source Bitcoin miner monitoring application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data MineViewer accesses and how it is handled.

## Data Collection

**MineViewer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

All data stays on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no telemetry.

## Local Network Access

MineViewer requires access to your local network to discover and communicate with Bitcoin mining devices (such as BitAxe, Antminer, and similar hardware). This communication happens directly between your device and your miners over your local Wi-Fi network. No data leaves your network.

## Internet Access

MineViewer connects to the following public APIs to display market data:

- **CoinGecko API** (api.coingecko.com) -- for cryptocurrency prices
- **Mempool.space API** (mempool.space) -- for Bitcoin network difficulty and hashrate

These requests do not include any personal information or device identifiers. They are standard public API calls to retrieve market data.

## Data Storage

All miner configurations, monitoring history, and app settings are stored locally on your device. MineViewer does not use cloud storage or external databases.

## Third-Party Services

MineViewer does not integrate with any advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers. The app contains no ads and no in-app purchases.

## Open Source

MineViewer is fully open source. You can inspect the entire codebase to verify these privacy claims at: https://github.com/mineviewer/mineviewer

## Children's Privacy

MineViewer does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and include the updated policy in the next app release.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository at https://github.com/robjobbed/mineviewer/issues.
